UKICER – The UK and Ireland
Computing Education Research Conference

UKICER 2019, the first research conference of the UK and Ireland ACM Special Interest Group in Computer Science Education was held at the University of Kent at Canterbury, United Kingdom, hosted by the University of Kent.

The conference took place on Thursday 5th September and Friday 6th September 2019. The main conference running from lunch time on the Thursday to lunch time on the Friday with workshops on Friday afternoon.

The programme

The conference took place in Darwin conference suite.The full conference programme is available here.

The conference proceedings are available in the ACM digital library.

ACM-Women UK Inspire 2019

We were pleased to host the 2019 ACM-Women UK Inspire Workshop.

Who is it for?

UKICER is aimed at practitioners and researchers in computing education, both within Computing departments and elsewhere. The conference provides a platform to share and discuss research in computing education.
